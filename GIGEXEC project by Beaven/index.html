About GigExec

GigExec is the new way for founders and startups to hire and get professional help from African industry experts.

A wide range of experts are available for very low consultancy rates, with a considerable fraction being donated to those in need.

How GigExec Works

For Founders & Startups

Gig Exec allows founders to use the experience of seasoned African professionals to allow for skipping of the discovery phase in startups. Founders hire executives to get advice and guidance for a small fee.

For Executives

Executives are showcased on the platform and are hired to serve as consultants for founders and startups. A percentage of the hiring fee is donated to charities around Africa. Hiring fees are dependant on the executive's exposure.
